Places to Visit in Maysville

Places to Visit in Maysville: EAT Gallery

EAT Gallery features jewelry from goldsmiths across America. All items of jewelry are one-of-a-kind and are hand fabricated in gold, platinum, silver and other precious metals.They also showcase rare and unusual gemstones and pearls from around the globe. Our own collection of gemstone beads featuring stones, mother-of-pearl, lava and coral has to be seen to be believed. Every color, hue and shape is represented in necklaces and bracelets of varying lengths and designs. The variety of natural cultured pearls that you will see will open your minds way past those round white pearls of grandmas! There is also an incredible array of hand carvings in stone. One piece, portraying a family of Iguanas, took 250 days to complete and weighs in excess of 100 lbs. All around the gallery you will also find sparkling mineral specimens of Citrine and Amethyst from the mines of South America and fossils from the Atlas Mountains of Morocco.

Fontana used to work for the EAT Gallery and the couple had their engagement ring and wedding bands made there.

Places to Visit in Maysville: Kentucky Gateway Museum Center

The Kentucky Gateway Museum Center in Maysville, Kentucky, combines history, art, and research to create one of the most unique collections of Kentucky records, artifacts, memorabilia, and art pieces in the Commonwealth. The KYGMC features the Regional History Museum, which contains more than 4,000 regional artifacts, dioramas, and exhibits; the distinguished Genealogical & Historical Research Library, and the KSB Miniatures Collection, one of the most notable collections of fine art miniatures in the world.
